Why becoming a language immersion host with Abroadwith?

If you have been hosting international students at home you already know from first experience how rewarding hospitality and sharing your language and culture with others can be. These are some of the added benefits from becoming a host at Abroadwith:

  • Find a family/homestay to immerse in the local culture and learn the language in a native environment
  • Learn about the families, hosts and students apartments before booking with them
  • Find immersions with a common lifestyle
  • Find the most convenient location
  • Immersions with better prices than conventional agencies, no intermediates
  • Option to have an immersion without leaving your city

Immersion options

Hosts are expected to make students feel at home. All immersion programs include breakfast, and a minimum of 7 hours spent with students to immerse them in the local culture and language.

Stay: in this traditional homestay program, share your home with foreign language students and make them part of your community. Students live at home like another member of the family or the student apartment taking part in the daily life and activities at home. Immersion hosts should welcome the students at home and encourage them to speak the local language as often as possible.

Tandem: what if instead of going abroad to learn a language you could bring that language home?This is the main idea behind tandem, share your home with foreign language students and learn a language with them. In this immersion program students and hosts split the amount of hours learning between each tandem language. Choose what languages are you interested in learning on your home admin and start learning. Both students and hosts are encouraged to take initiative in communicating as much as possible and correct each other to help improve language skills and make the immersion program a success for everyone.

Teacher's stay: if you are a qualified teacher, you can start teaching foreign language students at home, offering a Tearcher’s Stay immersion program. Teachers are expected to go beyond welcoming students like a member of the family or apartment. Talking to students frequently and encourage them to speak in the local language is important in every immersion program. Being a teacher stay means to go an extra mile and take responsibility in making a successful language immersion program.

In this option Teachers Choose how many hours a week you want to teach students (5, 10 or 15), decide your hourly lesson’s rate and get ready to teach. In order to offer a Teacher stay, Teacher must provide Abroadwith with enough evidence to get certified, different teaching certifications are accepted depending on the taught language.
