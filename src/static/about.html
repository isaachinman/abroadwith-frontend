A one stop platform for language and cultural exchange

Founded in August 2015 and based in Berlin, Abroadwith is an open community marketplace for people to learn languages immersing in other cultures.

Living with a traditional Chinese family learning their language and about their culture is without a doubt an exciting and life changing experience. Finding one to host you while living on the opposite side of the world can be hindering. Communication is lack there of, especially when you’re not yet fluent in the language.

Spending a summer in the lush green countryside of Ireland with a local family is an ideal way to improve your English. When the commute to the provided language school is 45 minutes away, however, furthering your studies becomes inconvenient.

It’s because of anecdotes like these that Abroadwith came to be. To tremendously improve communication between students and hosts. No matter the distance. No matter the language.

Abroadwith is the first one-stop platform where language learners can connect with their ideal host, find their preferred language course and book!
