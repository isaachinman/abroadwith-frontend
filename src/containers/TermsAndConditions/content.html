2. Binding Agreement

Abroadwith is a platform through which cultural immersion and language exchange is facilitated by allowing immersion hosts and language schools offer up their services. These services are booked directly by the students from the immersion hosts and educators on the platform. We act as the interface between the immersion hosts, educators and students.

Students enter into a contract, when they send an immersion booking request. Immersion hosts and educators enter into a contract, when they confirm a booking request. The terms, conditions and obligations of this contract are specified below:

The student discloses what kind of immersion option he/she is requesting, including the duration of the immersion, the type of immersion program, details concerning the immersion option and services requested at home to the immersion host. The student must abide by the price disclosed publicly set forth by the immersion host, as well as Abroadwith’s Service fee.

Additionally, the student may book a language course from a language school or a language tutor (educators) in proximity to their host's home. In this case, the student lets the educator know what kind of language course option he/she is requesting, including the duration of the course, course schedule and level. The student must abide by the price disclosed publicly by the educators, as well as Abroadwith's fees.

The contract between students and immersion host does not go into effect, if the immersion hosts denies the request during the confirmation period, which is within the first 48 hours after a booking request is made. Additionally, the contract does not go into effect, if the immersion hosts fails to respond to the request during the confirmation period. In such case as this, the request is automatically denied.

The contract between students and educators does not go into effect, if the educator denies the request during the confirmation period, which is within in the first 72 hours after a booking request is made. Additionally the contract does not go into effect, if the educator fails to respond to the request during the confirmation period. In such case as this, the request is automatically denied.

If the immersion hosts confirms the request, then the immersion programme becomes payable as agreed upon previously. Language courses offered by educators are only payable after the immersion programme has been confirmed by the host.

The contract between immersion hosts and educators with students is established the moment an immersion booking and/or language course request is/are confirmed. Language course request can only be confirmed by the educator once immersion has been approved by the immersion host.

This acceptance of a booking request entails a binding declaration by immersion hosts and educators to provide a service as specified on the immersion programme and language course request respectively. Including, but not limited to:

1) In the case of the immersion program, the immersion option, duration of the immersion, accommodation details, details concerning the immersion option, services requested at home, pricing of the service.

2) In the case of the educators, the duration of the course, course content, schedule, level and pricing of the course.

10. Cancellation

Student has the right to withdraw before the service is fully executed, that is before the immersion and or language course has been approved. After approval standard withdrawal is no longer possible and cancelation can be requested before the start of the programme / language course incurring the penalties described below.

After immersion programme and/or language course have been approved by immersion hosts and/or educators any of the parties may cancel, including students. In such cases the following penalties apply:

1) Student Cancellation:

  • Standard (over 14 days before the immersion programme or language course begins). Students pay the assigned booking fees.
  • Short notice (less than 14 days to start date). Student is penalised with a fixed rate of 15% (including all fees).

2) Immersion Host Cancellation:

  • Standard (over 14 days before the immersion programme begins). Full refund, minus 3% transaction costs.
  • Short notice (less than 14 days to start date). Full refund, minus 3% transaction costs. Host is penalised with a fixed rate 10% deductible from their next booking.

3) Educator Cancellation:

  • Standard (over 14 days before the language course starts). Full refund, minus 3% transaction costs.
  • Short notice (less than 14 days to start date). Full refund, minus 3% transaction costs. Educator is penalised with a fixed rate 10% deductible from their next booking.
